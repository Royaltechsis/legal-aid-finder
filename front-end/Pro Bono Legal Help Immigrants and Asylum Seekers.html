Pro Bono Legal Help Immigrants and Asylum Seekers

Seeking asylum is a complex legal process, and many refugees face deportation due to a lack of legal representation. A young mother, Mama Peace, fled her home country due to political persecution. She had no legal knowledge or resources to navigate the asylum process. A team of pro bono immigration attorneys helped her: Prepare her asylum application Present her case in court Gain legal residency

Today, she and her children live safely in a new country, free from fear.
