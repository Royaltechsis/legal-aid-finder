Legal Help for a Woman Seeking a Divorce from an Abusive Spouse

Breaking Free from Abuse

Marriage should be built on love and respect, but for Mercy, it became a nightmare of fear and suffering. Trapped in an abusive relationship with no financial means to hire a lawyer, she struggled to find a way out.

How Legal Aid Helped

A dedicated pro bono family law attorney stepped in to help her escape by:

  • Filing for divorce and securing a restraining order
  • Winning full custody of her children
  • Helping her access financial support and safe housing

A New Beginning

With legal assistance, she was able to break free and start a new life in safety, providing a better future for herself and her children.
