Welcome To Justice Seeker Legal Aid

We connect users with qualified lawyers who offer pro bono(free) legal representation.

By offering a wide range of services and advocating for systemic change,

we strive to create a just and equitable society where everyone's legal rights are protected and upheld.

About Us

Our Mission

To ensure free, effective and efficient legal aid services to indigents in Nigeria

Our Vision

To build a nation where there is equal access to justice for all irrespective of means

and where all rights are respected, protected and defended.
